Tattoo Aftercare Instructions

Proper care is essential for a beautiful, long-lasting tattoo. Please follow these instructions carefully.

Day 1: Initial Care

  • **Leave the bandage on** for the time recommended (usually 3-4 hours, or overnight for Saniderm/Dermalize).
  • **Wash your hands** thoroughly before touching your new tattoo.
  • Gently **wash the tattoo** with mild, unscented soap and lukewarm water. Do not scrub.
  • **Pat dry** with a clean, soft paper towel. Do not use a cloth towel.
  • Apply a very thin layer of **unscented, water-based lotion** or a product recommended by your artist. Do not over-moisturize.

Days 2-14: Healing Period

  • **Repeat the cleaning and moisturizing** 2-3 times per day.
  • It is normal to experience **some redness, swelling, and light scabbing/flaking**. Do not pick or scratch the scabs.
  • **Avoid soaking** the tattoo (no baths, pools, hot tubs) for at least 2-4 weeks. Quick showers are fine.
  • **Avoid direct sun exposure**. The sun is the biggest enemy of a new tattoo. Wear loose clothing to cover it if going outside.
  • **Wear loose-fitting clothes** to prevent friction on the tattooed area.
  • **Avoid intense workouts** that cause excessive sweating for the first few days, and always wash the tattoo immediately after any workout.

Long-Term Care

  • Always apply **sunscreen (SPF 30+)** to your tattoo when exposed to the sun, even after it is fully healed, to keep the colors vibrant.
  • Keep your skin **hydrated** with regular moisturizing.
  • If you notice signs of infection (severe redness, increasing pain, thick discharge, fever), **contact your artist and a doctor immediately.**

⚠️ Important Note

Every person heals differently. If you have any specific concerns about your healing process, please do not hesitate to **contact Meg directly**.
